Privacy Policy

Data Collection & Usage

This application (NUR) does not collect or store your personal data on our servers. All operations are performed strictly on your device (client-side).

Location Data

The app requests access to your precise location (Latitude/Longitude) solely to calculate prayer times and display weather. This data is used only for real-time queries to 3rd party APIs (Aladhan API, OpenMeteo) and is never saved in our databases.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your settings (language, theme, tasbih count, etc.) to enhance your user experience.

Third-Party Services

This app utilizes external services: Aladhan (Prayer Times), AlQuran Cloud (Quran Data), and OpenMeteo (Weather). Their respective privacy policies apply.
